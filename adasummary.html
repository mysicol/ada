who was ada lovelace?

Ada Lovelace is usually attributed with being the first computer scientist. Which is insane, because she lived 30 years prior to the invention of the lightbulb!

Ada Lovelace's contributions were mainly theoretical. She worked with Charles Babbage to design the Analytical Engine - which, although never created, is widely recognized as being the world's first computer.

That is because the Analytical Engine was Turing Complete, meaning it had important capabilities like abstraction and branching reasoning which made it as powerful as a modern-day computer.
